Faqs

What are the Pension Calculator and Budget Planner all about?

The Pension Calculator and Budget Planner are designed to help guide your financial decisions in order to assist you to meet your retirement objectives.

By visualising the impact of your financial decisions, the tools would provide a sense of direction as to what you should undertake financially to meet your long-term goals.

Why do I need to provide personal details?

The personal details determine what your estimated monthly pension would be at retirement. Pension payments are determined by four factors: your age at retirement, gender, last salary and the value of your RSA balance at the time of your application. These parameters are important.
